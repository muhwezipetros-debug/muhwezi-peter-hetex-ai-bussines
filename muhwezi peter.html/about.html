Muhwezi Peter

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Muhwezi Peter founded HETEX AI in 2015 with a revolutionary vision: to create artificial intelligence that would become the fundamental layer of all human technological progress. His journey began in Kampala, Uganda, where he recognized the transformative potential of AI for emerging markets.

With a background in computer science and machine learning, Peter assembled a world-class team of researchers and engineers to build what would become the world's most advanced AI platform. His leadership has positioned HETEX AI as the global market leader, surpassing established competitors and setting new standards in artificial intelligence.

Our Journey to Global Dominance

2015

Foundation in Kampala

HETEX AI was founded by Muhwezi Peter in Uganda as the first African AI company with breakthrough neural architecture research. Started with 5 engineers and a vision to democratize AI.

2017

First Major Breakthrough

Launched Core AI platform, processing 1M+ daily requests. Secured partnerships with African tech hubs and began global expansion. Alafi Jonathan joined as Co-Founder.

2019

Global Recognition

Reached unicorn status and secured partnerships with Amazon, Tesla, and Google. Opened offices in Silicon Valley, London, and Singapore. Expanded leadership team with Asiimwe Enoth and Korochu Emmanuel.

2022

Market Leadership

Became the world's leading AI provider with 85% market share in enterprise AI. Surpassed OpenAI as the most advanced AI platform globally. Strengthened operations with Namukwaya Florence and cybersecurity with Feni Horace.
